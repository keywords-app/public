Keywords.app

A focused iOS companion for performance marketers who live in Google Ads. Connect securely, explore your campaigns on‑device, and keep your data where it belongs.

View on the App Store Contact support

Keywords.app uses Google OAuth with limited scopes (profile email and Google Ads) and keeps your Google Ads campaign data on your device. Tokens are stored encrypted; campaign performance data is not stored or aggregated on our servers.
