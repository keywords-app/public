How it works

From connected to optimized in four steps

1

Connect your account

Sign in with Google. We pull your search terms with read access and limited scopes.

2

AI groups your terms

Review clusters grouped by meaning and intent instead of scrolling hundreds of terms. If one term in a cluster is waste, they usually all are.

3

Keep or block

Tag whole clusters at once, or keep and block any single term — with search, filters, and suggested negatives to move fast.

4

Push negatives, safely

The negative engine builds the shortest list that clears your blocked terms without ever hitting a keeper or a keyword you bid on — then pushes straight to Google Ads.

End to end: about 10 minutes. Average savings: ~10%.
