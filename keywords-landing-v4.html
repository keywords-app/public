The problem

Search term review takes too long.

So it gets skipped. Or done less often than it should be. Meanwhile your clients are paying for clicks that will never convert. That's not a discipline problem — it's a tooling problem.

The native Google Ads interface wasn't built for this workflow. You're bouncing between campaigns, scrolling the same terms over and over, typing out negatives one by one. It's slow by design. Keywords.app fixes the process.

How it works

Three things that change the process.

01

Triage at lightning speed

Whip through your search terms list sorting good from bad. Block the bad ones with the Negative Match Maker — faster than anything you've used before, with little or no typing required.

02

AI does the pre-work

Keywords.app groups your search terms into clusters by searcher intent and makes recommendations. 90% of the job becomes "yes, I agree." You focus on the edge cases that actually need judgment.

03

Track exactly what you saved

Keywords.app tracks the wasted spend you eliminated. Show your boss or your client exactly how much you saved them — with real numbers, not estimates.

The compounding effect

It never asks you about the same term twice.

Every decision you make gets remembered. Next time you open it, you're only looking at new terms — and the AI has already pre-sorted those. The list gets shorter and faster every single session.
