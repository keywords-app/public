Common Questions

How do I connect my Google Ads account?

Sign in with Google on the welcome screen. Keywords.app will request access to your Google Ads accounts. You can connect multiple Google logins if you manage accounts under different emails.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your campaign data stays on your device. We don't store or aggregate your Google Ads data on our servers. OAuth tokens are encrypted.

What can I do with Keywords.app?

Check campaign performance, enable/pause campaigns and keywords, triage search terms, and build negative keywords — all from your phone.

How do I disconnect my Google account?

Go to your Google account settings at myaccount.google.com, find "Third-party apps with account access," and revoke access for Keywords.app.
