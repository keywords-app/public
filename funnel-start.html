How it works
1
AI fetches and groups your search terms

Connect your Google Ads account and our AI reads your search terms, then organizes them into clusters based on meaning and intent.

2
Tag good and bad clusters

Review organized clusters instead of individual terms. If one term in a cluster is bad, they're usually all bad. Negative keyword suggestions make it even faster.

3
Block the bad, keep the good

Our Negative Match Maker pushes your negative keywords to Google, locking in your savings for life. No typing required.

Track your savings live

Estimated savings (typically >10%) shows live in the app as you work. Show your boss or client exactly what you saved.
