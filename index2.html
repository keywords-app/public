The Problem

Hundreds of search terms across multiple campaigns. Each one needs a decision. Each negative keyword added manually. It takes an hour minimum. So it gets pushed to next week.

Meanwhile, your clients are paying for clicks on "free," "cheap," and "how to do it myself."

One beta tester cut over a third off their new client's spend in less than 10 minutes with keywords.app.

How It Works

1

Swipe Through Search Terms

Left to block. Right to keep. Every search term triggering your ads. No menus, no campaign structures. Just swipe.

2

Generate Negative Matches

The negative match generator turns your blocked terms into negative keywords in seconds. No more typing them out one by one.

3

Push To Google Ads

One tap sends negatives straight to Google Ads. Campaign, ad group, or account level. The app remembers every decision — next review, you only see new terms.

Features

  • 📊 Check performance and pause or enable campaigns, ad groups, and keywords on the go
  • 🧭 Navigate accounts, campaigns, ad groups, and keywords with full metrics at every level
  • 👆 Swipe to keep or block search terms — the app remembers so you never review the same term twice
  • 🚫 Generate negative keywords from blocked terms in seconds and push directly to Google Ads
  • ⚠️ Conflict detection warns before you accidentally block a search term that's making you money
  • 🔒 Your data stays on your device — Google OAuth with limited scopes, campaign data never stored on our servers

Privacy

Keywords.app uses Google OAuth with limited scopes (profile, email, and Google Ads). All campaign data is stored on your device — not on our servers, not in the cloud. Tokens are stored encrypted. Campaign performance data is never stored or aggregated on our servers. Your clients' data stays yours.
